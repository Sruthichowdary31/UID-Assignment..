About Scoops

“Someday,” that was the day that Susan Kirk thought she would get the opportunity to open an ice cream shop of her own. That day came when the perfect location for the first Scoops became available in downtown Madison, GA.

Madison is a picture-perfect rural community once named America’s best small town. Along the tree-lined streets, Madison had its share of history, culture, and recreation but no ice cream shop. Hence, “Someday,” Ms. Susan’s vision of an ice cream shop was one like none other.

From homemade fudge and chocolates to hand-crafted ice cream flavors, Scoops became a favorite destination for families and sweet lovers. Today Scoops continues to grow with multiple locations and franchise opportunities.
